Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

Gambeta ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to preventing and combating child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in all forms. This policy states our publicly published standards against CSAE.

Last updated: January 2025

1. Zero tolerance

We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation and for child sexual abuse material (CSAM). We do not permit any content or conduct on Gambeta that:

  • Depicts, promotes, or facilitates child sexual abuse or exploitation
  • Constitutes or depicts CSAM (including AI-generated or synthetic CSAM)
  • Sexualizes minors in any way
  • Facilitates grooming, sextortion, or trafficking of children

Such content and conduct are strictly prohibited and will be removed. We will take action against accounts and report to appropriate authorities where required by law.

2. Prohibited conduct

The following are explicitly prohibited on Gambeta:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM): Any content that depicts or promotes the sexual abuse or exploitation of minors, including real, simulated, or AI-generated imagery or descriptions.
  • Grooming: Building a relationship or trust with a minor for the purpose of sexual abuse, exploitation, or coercion.
  • Sextortion: Coercing or threatening someone (including minors) to produce sexual content or to comply with sexual demands.
  • Sexualization of minors: Content or behavior that sexualizes anyone under 18, including inappropriate comments, requests, or imagery.
  • Child trafficking: Any conduct that facilitates the trafficking of children for sexual or other exploitation.

3. How we enforce this policy

We enforce this policy through:

  • Content moderation: Review and removal of reported or detected content that violates this policy.
  • Account action: Warnings, suspension, or permanent termination of accounts that violate this policy or applicable law.
  • Reporting to authorities: Where we have a good-faith belief that content or conduct involves CSAM or child exploitation, we report to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and/or law enforcement as required by applicable law (including 18 U.S.C. § 2258A and equivalent obligations in other jurisdictions).

4. How to report

If you see content or behavior on Gambeta that you believe involves child sexual abuse, exploitation, or CSAM, please report it immediately.

  • In the app: Use the report option on the relevant content, conversation, or profile, and select the option that best describes the issue (e.g., child safety / exploitation).
  • By email: Contact support@gambetaapp.com with the subject line "CSAE Report" or "Child Safety Report." Include as much detail as you can (e.g., screenshots, usernames, links) without sharing or forwarding the abusive content itself.

We treat CSAE reports as urgent and will act on them in line with this policy and our legal obligations.

5. Child safety contact

For app store, partner, or regulatory inquiries regarding Gambeta's child safety practices or this policy, you may contact:

Email: support@gambetaapp.com Subject line: Child Safety / CSAE Inquiry

We will route such inquiries to the appropriate team for a timely response.

6. Related policies

This CSAE policy works together with our Terms of Service (https://gambetaapp.com/terms) and Privacy Policy (https://gambetaapp.com/privacy). Violations of this policy may also breach those terms and can result in account action and, where appropriate, referral to law enforcement.

Gambeta is committed to keeping our platform safe and to meeting our responsibilities under applicable child safety and CSAE laws and standards.
